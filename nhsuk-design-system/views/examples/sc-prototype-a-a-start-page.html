Draft hypothesis

We believe that a orientation feature on the level 1 hub page will

  • Help those people who don’t understand what we mean by ‘carer’
  • Help those people who aren’t sure what care and support options there are available to them
  • Help those people who aren’t sure what care and support entitlements there are
  • Help those people who don’t know who delivers the care and support
    • and who to speak when they have a need
    • and know who to apply with when they have a need
